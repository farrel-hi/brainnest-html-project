  • About

    J-pop is an abbreviated term for Japanese pop or Japanese pop music. J-pop is a musical genre that has been a part of Japanese pop culture since the mid-twentieth century. J-pop applies to various music genres, from jazz and vocal ballads to pop-rock and folk, and idol pop. More..

  • History

    The history of J-pop begins in the early years of the Showa era (the 1920s to 1980s) with ryūkōka, a form of popular music influenced by Western jazz and blues. More..
